OUR STORY

The story of Olubi Construction Company Limited begins in 2007, when it was incorporated as a private limited liability company in Osogbo, Osun State, Nigeria. The company was founded with the goal of delivering reliable civil and building construction services across the country. From its early days, Olubi Construction focused on road works, building projects, and engineering services, growing steadily through contracts with both state and federal governments, as well as institutions and private organizations. They also invested in their own block industry and construction equipment, which gave them independence and efficiency compared to smaller contractors. Over the years, the company became recognized for its timely project delivery, zero history of abandonment, and a strong reputation for quality and integrity. Major clients such as the Federal Road Maintenance Agency (FERMA), Obafemi Awolowo University, and UBA Bank have relied on them for critical infrastructure projects. Today, Olubi Construction’s story is one of growth from a regional contractor into a respected company with over 18 years of proven experience, managed by a skilled leadership team and supported by a full fleet of modern construction equipment.
