Who We Are

Olubi Construction Company Limited is a private limited liability company incorporated in 2007 with headquarters in Osogbo, Osun State, Nigeria. We specialize in building and civil engineering construction, estate development, electrical and mechanical works, and procurement services. For over 18 years, we have delivered projects for federal and state governments, institutions, and private clients across Nigeria — building a reputation for quality, integrity, and timely delivery.

Our Edge

  • Zero history of project abandonment or litigation.
  • Proven track record with clients like FERMA, Obafemi Awolowo University, Cocoa Research Institute, and UBA Bank.
  • A strong technical team supported by a fleet of modern equipment.
