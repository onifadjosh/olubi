SERVICES OVERVIEW

At Olubi Construction, we combine expertise, modern equipment, and skilled professionals to deliver projects across civil engineering, building construction, and procurement services. Our wide range of services ensures quality from design to completion.

Building & Civil Engineering Construction

We specialize in the design and execution of major civil and building works, including roads, bridges, culverts, drain channels, and reinforced concrete structures. Our team has successfully delivered projects for both federal and state governments, ensuring durability, safety, and compliance with engineering standards.

Electrical & Mechanical Works.

Our expertise covers the installation of electrical and mechanical systems for residential, commercial, and industrial projects. From heavy-duty foundations and piling to special roofing solutions, we deliver projects with precision and reliability, ensuring long-term functionality.

Finishing & Landscaping

We provide high-quality finishing services such as aluminum works, interlocking paving, landscaping, and swimming pool construction. Our goal is to create environments that are not only functional but also aesthetically pleasing, adding value to every project.

Design & Project Delivery

Olubi Construction offers complete project delivery services including design-and-build, management contracting, and subcontracting works. Our integrated approach allows us to handle projects from concept to completion, ensuring cost efficiency, timeliness, and top-quality outcomes.

Materials & Procurement

We operate our own block industry and quarries, supplying asphalt, granite, stone base, laterite, and sand to meet construction needs. This in-house capacity ensures a steady supply of quality materials, reducing project delays and maintaining high standards.
